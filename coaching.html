what is private coaching?

Private coaching allows you to create your own team and send invite codes to your players. This is the perfect option for coaches who already have a team or want to build one from scratch.

features:

  • Send team invite codes to your players
  • Free team management features (chat, schedule)
  • Set your own team fee rate
  • Provide personalized training through the app
  • Review player submissions and give feedback

üí° All coaches must start with private coaching before becoming a public coach.

üåç public coaching

Expand your reach and coach players from around the world who aren't on a team using TeamsFC.

what is public coaching?

Public coaching allows individual players without a team to sign up under your coaching. These are players who want personalized training but aren't part of an organized team.

requirements:

  • Complete 50 reviews as a private coach first
  • Maintain high coaching standards
  • Pass additional verification

benefits:

  • Access to players actively seeking coaches
  • Expand your coaching business
  • Build your reputation on the platform
  • Same pricing flexibility as private coaching
