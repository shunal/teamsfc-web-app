frequently asked questions

how do i get started with teamsfc?
download the TEAMSFC app from the app store or google play store, create your profile, and start with our beginner challenges. our system will automatically assess your skill level and provide personalized training recommendations.
is teamsfc suitable for all skill levels?
yes! TEAMSFC is designed for players of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. our adaptive system provides challenges and feedback appropriate for your current level.
how do the prizes and rewards work?
complete challenges and evaluations to earn points and unlock rewards. top performers can win real prizes, exclusive avatars, and recognition on our leaderboards.
can coaches join the platform?
absolutely! we welcome qualified coaches to join our platform. coaches can earn income by providing evaluations and personalized feedback to players. contact us for more information about coach registration.
is my data secure?
yes, we take data security seriously. all personal information and video content are encrypted and stored securely. we never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
