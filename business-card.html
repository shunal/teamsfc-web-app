TeamsFC Business Card

Front Side: Features your logo, company name, and QR code for easy app download.

Back Side: Contains download information and contact details.

How to Use:

  • Print at Home: Print on cardstock (3.5" x 2") and cut out
  • Professional Printing: Save as PDF and send to printing service
  • Digital Sharing: Use in emails or social media

QR Code:

✅ Local Development: QR code links to localhost:3000/downloads.html

✅ Production: Use qr-code-production.png for teamsfc.com/downloads.html

People can scan the QR code to instantly access your app download page.

Print Settings:

  • Size: 3.5" x 2" (standard business card)
  • Paper: Cardstock or premium paper
  • Print both sides for complete card
