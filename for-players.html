how it works

01
choose level
Easy, Medium, or Hard. Each level has different rep requirements and time limits.
02
complete challenges
Complete required reps within 20 seconds. Advanced AI verifies rep count and analyzes movement patterns to ensure proper execution and prevent cheating.
03
earn cards
Each completed challenge earns collectible cards. Build your collection as you progress through different categories.
04
unlock progression
Pass challenges to unlock the next level. Progress through categories systematically.
05
build decks
Use collected cards to build tactical decks. Compete in matches where your training directly impacts performance.
06
compete weekly
Ranked by rep-to-time ratio on global leaderboards. Resets weekly.
